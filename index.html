Deep System for Android Byte-Code Malware Detection and Attribution

Android, a revolutionary open-source mobile platform, powers millions of devices and applications. However, its rapid expansion has led to a surge in malware, with 99% of smartphone threats targeting Android. Existing malware detectors are struggling to keep pace with evolving threats, leaving users vulnerable. To combat this, we propose an anti-malware system leveraging deep customized learning models.

Denoising histopathology images for the detection of breast cancer

Breast cancer, a leading cause of mortality worldwide, necessitates automated histopathology image analysis tools due to time-consuming manual diagnosis. Our proposed system utilizes CNN-based architectures for denoising, detecting, and classifying breast cancer cells, improving diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.
